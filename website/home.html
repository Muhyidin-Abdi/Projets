MORE ART

Bristol has a rich and diverse art history that spans centuries, contributing significantly to the cultural landscape of the United Kingdom. Known for its vibrant street art scene, Bristol gained international acclaim as the hometown of the elusive graffiti artist Banksy. The city's artistic heritage extends beyond street art, encompassing a wide range of styles and movements
